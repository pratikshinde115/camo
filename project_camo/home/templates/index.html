World's First & Only Instant Cashless Hospitalisation Card

India's groundbreaking solution for seamless, cashless hospital admittance during medical emergencies. Gone are the days of navigating through tedious pre-authorization processes. With CAMO Card, accessing medical care is as simple as a swipe. CAMO Card revolutionizes the way individuals handle medical occurrences by offering instant cashless admittance to hospitals across India. No more worries about carrying large sums of cash or dealing with complicated insurance paperwork during emergencies. Our innovative application ensures that users can swiftly access the care they need without any hassle. With just a swipe of the CAMO Card, hospitalization becomes a seamless process, allowing individuals to focus on what truly matters - their health and well-being. Experience peace of mind knowing that CAMO Card has your back during life's unexpected moments.

Awesome Features

Truly Cashless Card

CAMO Card redefines cashless convenience in the realm of medical emergencies by eliminating the need for physical currency or complex insurance processes

Emergency Financial Assistant

In critical situations, CAMO Card serves as a reliable financial lifeline, allowing users to access essential medical services without the burden of immediate payment, thereby prioritizing their health and well-being above financial concerns.

Know your Policy

The CAMO app leverages Artificial Intelligence (AI) technology, which is capable of scanning insurance policies and providing policyholders with adequate and precise information to avoid claim rejections

Bundled Benefits

Get discounted rates on Pharmacy Services, Tele-medicine, Preventive Health Check-ups, Doctor on Chat, Emergency Medical Assistance, Mental Health Support, Counselling Services and more...

Single Window Clearence & Support

CAMO team coordinates with your Insurance Service Provider & Third Party Administrator to provide the reinstatement of your insurance amount thereby reducing your financial liability towards the cost of hospitalisation

Complimentary Group Personal Accident Cover

Every CAMO Card holder is protected for INR 5 Lacs of Personal Accident Cover. This is provided incase of Accidental Death or Accidental Disability. T&C apply

Instant Activation
Subscription

You can get your CAMO Card through your Company or by Subscribing it online via camocard.in

Activation

Upon the receipt of the card, You need to download CAMO App on your Smart Phone. Upload your Aadhaar, PAN & click on 'Activate Card'

Linking

Upload & Link your Insurance Policy for Instant Cashless Activation

Utilization

Select the 'Network Hospital' from the Drop Down on the CAMO App. Follow the process & swipe your card instantly

Reinstatement

You can reinstate your credit amount by converting your outstanding to EMI or by settlement of claim

How It Works

3 Easy Steps

Install the App

Begin your journey with CAMO by downloading our app from our website camocard.in. Once downloaded, open the app to start experiencing the convenience and benefits it offers.

Setup Your Profile

After installing the app, it's time to set up your profile. This step is quick and easy. Provide your basic information such as your name, date of birth, contact details, and upload your KYC as any additional information required. You may also upload your existing Insurance Policy Copy. This helps us personalize your CAMO experience and ensure that you have access to all our features

Enjoy The Features

With your profile set up, you're ready to dive into the world of CAMO! Whether you're looking for cashless hospital admittance, access to telemedicine services, or discounts on wellness activities, CAMO has you covered. Explore our range of features designed to make managing your healthcare needs easier and more convenient. From instant approvals to hassle-free payments, enjoy peace of mind knowing that CAMO is here to support you every step of the way
